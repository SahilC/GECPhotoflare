welcome!
PhotoFlare is an international photography festival organised by the Photography Club of Goa College of Engineering, Goa, India. It consists of an international competition, an exhibition and talks and workshops.

We've grown a lot over the last 3 years, and now, we're bigger, better and flashier than ever before! So join us one more time, armed with your cameras, to celebrate the joys of clickography.

competition

It's said that the world is a different place as seen through a Photographer’s lens. The skill to compose a shot, to see the extra-ordinary in everyday stuff is a gift only a few possess. However, it’s a cultivated gift. A skill that is honed by continuously challenging oneself, by pushing one’s own self in a corner and striving to move ahead. At PhotoFlare, we push you in such a corner. Following are the themes for PhotoFlare2013.
You can submit your entries here.

forgotten

We live a fast paced life; one where every day brings with it new possibilities. But in this new way of life, have we left something behind?

noise

Noise is all around is. It could be in the form of the incessant drone of traffic or the visual mess of cluttered road signs or billboards. How do you see noise? Show us.

in my dreams

We are very particular about the entries we receive being un-edited in every way. But we thought "What the hell, almost everyone edits their photos nowadays!". So here's presenting PhotoFlare's first ever image-manipulation based theme! Show us how creative you can get with your image editing skills.

music video

PhotoFlare's first stop-motion based theme. Stop motion is not only fun, but is comparatively easy to make and lets you get really creative. Make a stop-motion video of atleast 30 seconds based on a song of your choice, and send us the link to where ever you have uploaded it.

expo

The showcase event of PhotoFlare 2013 will be the expo. Nominated photos from each category will be displayed at the Kala Academy, Panjim-Goa on the 10th and 11th of May, 2013. about

Started by a bunch of amateur photographers, we are the Photography Club of Goa Engineering College, Farmagudi, Goa. United by our love for Photography, we meet once in a while to discuss Photography, click pictures and learn from each other. PhotoFlare is a manifestation of our collective love for everything photography. A chance to bring together Photographers from varied backgrounds and organize an event that gives a chance to showcase photographic talent and organize workshops, lectures and meetings which connect the amateurs to the professionals. This is our fourth year and we hope to continue our ascend towards becoming one of the most awaited Photography events of the year. Hope to see you at PhotoFlare 2013. Keep Clicking!
